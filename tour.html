🌍 Tour Uganda with MoC

Every weekend, Mayambala Orphanage Center opens the doors to discovery. We take our volunteers and children on unforgettable journeys—exploring Uganda’s vibrant villages, natural wonders, lakes, and heritage sites.

This is more than sightseeing—it’s about connection, joy, and shared culture.

🗓️ Weekend Adventure Schedule

Destination Highlights Day & Time
Lake Victoria Shores Picnic with children, bird watching, storytelling by the water Saturday, 10AM–2PM
Heritage Village Walk Visit cultural sites and learn about local traditions Saturday, 3PM–5PM
Faith & Fellowship Visit Attend a local church or community gathering Sunday Morning
Youth Dialogue Circle Group discussion with volunteers and teens Sunday, 2PM–4PM
