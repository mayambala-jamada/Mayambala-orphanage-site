🌍 Tour Uganda with MoC

Every weekend, Mayambala Orphanage Center opens the doors to discovery. We take volunteers and children on unforgettable journeys—exploring Uganda’s vibrant villages, lakes, and heritage sites.

This is more than sightseeing—it’s about connection, joy, and shared culture.

🗓️ Weekend Adventure Schedule

Destination Highlights Day & Time
Lake Victoria Shores Picnic, bird watching, storytelling Saturday, 10AM–2PM
Heritage Village Walk Cultural sites and local traditions Saturday, 3PM–5PM
Faith & Fellowship Visit Attend a church or community gathering Sunday Morning
Youth Dialogue Circle Group discussion with volunteers and teens Sunday, 2PM–4PM
