📊 Our Impact

Every child we reach, every volunteer who serves, and every seed we plant is part of a bigger story—one of transformation, resilience, and hope. Here's what your support has made possible.

🎒 150+ Children in School

Each term, we provide school fees, uniforms, and supplies—ensuring children stay in class and dream big.

🌿 400+ Trees Planted

Our June 2025 Tree Planting Day brought together youth and volunteers to green 3 villages with indigenous trees.

👩‍🍳 20 Women Trained

In May, mothers completed skills training in baking, soap-making, and tailoring—building income and dignity.

🧼 100+ Hygiene Kits Distributed

We provide soap, pads, and hygiene education to promote health and confidence among children and teens.

🌍 5+ Countries Represented

Volunteers from around the world have joined MoC—sharing skills, stories, and love across borders.
