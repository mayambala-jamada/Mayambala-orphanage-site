📘 Our Programs

MoC is powered by purpose. Our programs respond to the real needs of orphaned and vulnerable children—creating safe spaces, skills, and systems where they can grow in dignity and confidence.

📚 Education Support

We support over 150 children with school fees, uniforms, and supplies each term—ensuring no child is left behind because of poverty.

🌱 Farming & Nutrition

Through a growing community garden, children and caregivers plant, harvest, and learn sustainable farming skills that support both nutrition and learning.

🎨 Creative Arts for Healing

Children explore identity and joy through music, drawing, dance, storytelling, and theater—often guided by volunteer artists.

🧼 Hygiene & Health Awareness

We conduct hygiene workshops, distribute soap and pads, and work with medical volunteers to ensure every child is clean, confident, and cared for.

🙌 Faith & Mentorship

Every week, children gather for prayer, Bible-based lessons, and heart-to-heart talks—building spiritual resilience and emotional safety.
