📘 Our Programs

MoC’s programs respond to the real needs of orphaned and vulnerable children—creating safe spaces, skills, and systems where they can grow in dignity and confidence.

📚 Education Support

We support over 150 children with school fees, uniforms, and supplies each term—ensuring no child is left behind.

🌱 Farming & Nutrition

Children and caregivers plant, harvest, and learn sustainable farming skills that support nutrition and learning.

🎨 Creative Arts

Children explore identity and joy through music, drawing, dance, storytelling, and theater with volunteer artists.

🧼 Hygiene & Health

Workshops, soap distribution, and medical guidance ensure children are clean, confident, and cared for.

🙌 Faith & Mentorship

Weekly Bible lessons, prayers, and discussions build spiritual resilience and emotional safety.
