📘 Our Programs

MoC’s programs respond to the real needs of orphaned and vulnerable children—creating safe spaces, skills, and systems to thrive.

📚 Education Support

School fees, uniforms, and supplies for over 150 children each term.

🌱 Farming & Nutrition

Children learn sustainable farming while growing healthy food for the center.

🎨 Creative Arts

Music, dance, painting, storytelling, and theater to heal and inspire.

🧼 Hygiene & Health

Workshops, soap distribution, and medical support for children and caregivers.

🙌 Faith & Mentorship

Bible-based lessons, prayers, and mentorship sessions for emotional and spiritual growth.
