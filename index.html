Empowering Orphaned Children in Uganda

Mayambala Orphanage Center (MoC) is a faith-driven, community-rooted nonprofit providing education, shelter, mentorship, and creative space for over 150 vulnerable children in Kampala.

MoC isn’t powered by one—it’s driven by many.

Hope That Sustains

Join #RaiseHopeUganda as we nurture brighter futures—empowering vulnerable children while restoring the planet they call home. Together, we plant more than trees. We plant hope.
