🌱 Our Story

Mayambala Orphanage Center (MoC) was founded in 2018 in Badongo village, Makindye Division, Kampala. Driven by faith, compassion, and love for vulnerable children, MoC has grown into a beacon of hope.

💡 Our Mission

To nurture orphaned and vulnerable children by providing shelter, education, mentorship, and creative platforms that empower them to thrive and lead.

🕊️ Our Values

  • Compassion over convenience
  • Faith in action
  • Community-driven impact
  • Creativity as a tool for healing
  • Accountability to the lives we serve

📈 Fast Facts

  • Founded: 2018
  • Children Supported: 150+
  • Global Volunteer Network: 5+ countries
  • Partnerships: Schools, local farms, mentors
