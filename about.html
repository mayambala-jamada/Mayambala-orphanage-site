🌱 Our Story

Mayambala Orphanage Center (MoC) was founded in 2018 in Badongo village, Makindye Division, Kampala. Driven by faith, compassion, and a fierce love for vulnerable children, MoC began as a local response to community hardship—and has grown into a beacon of hope.

💡 Our Mission

To nurture orphaned and vulnerable children by providing shelter, education, mentorship, and creative platforms that empower them to thrive and lead. We believe every child deserves to be seen, heard, and supported.

🕊️ Our Values

  • Compassion over convenience
  • Faith in action
  • Community-driven impact
  • Creativity as a tool for healing and growth
  • Accountability to the lives we serve

📈 Fast Facts

  • Founded: 2018
  • Location: Badongo village, Makindye Division, Kampala – Uganda
  • Children Supported: Over 150 annually
  • Global Volunteer Network: 5+ countries represented
  • Partnerships: Schools, local farms, youth mentors
